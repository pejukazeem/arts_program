Why Study in Canada?

Canada has one of the best natural sceneries in the world and boasts of many outdoor activities throughouth the year. Thus, you can enjoy skiing in the winter and kayaking in the summer (note that the writer has never tried any of these activities in the country)

Although this large country has a population of 36 million, it is very diversed and you are bound to make friends and experience new cultures and cuisine.

The level of pollution is much lower than that of Nigeria. This means you will be spared from seeing mushroom clouds from your balcony. As well, no mad driver or bus conductor will be shouting at you on the road (although you will still experience road rage, some people just don't have sense).

Lastly, Canada is a land of opportunities. It offers its inhabitants an opportunity to try new things and explore their talents.
